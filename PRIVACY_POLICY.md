# Privacy Policy for YouTube Auto-Uploader

**Effective Date: November 2025**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how YouTube Auto-Uploader tools ("we," "our," or "the Service") collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **YouTube Account Information**: When you authenticate with the Service, we receive your YouTube channel ID and basic profile information through OAuth 2.0
- **OAuth Tokens**: We store access tokens and refresh tokens to maintain your authenticated session
- **Video Metadata**: Information about videos you upload, including titles, descriptions, tags, and settings
- **Configuration Settings**: Your preferences for upload settings, scheduling, and automation rules

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: Logs of Service usage, including upload times, API calls made, and errors encountered
- **Technical Information**: IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system (when applicable)
- **Performance Metrics**: Upload success rates, processing times, and error logs for service improvement

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties
- **YouTube Data**: Basic channel information and upload history from YouTube through the YouTube Data API
- **Google OAuth**: Authentication information from Google's OAuth 2.0 service

### 2.1 Service Provision

### 2.2 Service Improvement
- Monitor Service performance and reliability
- Identify and fix bugs or technical issues

### 2.3 Security and Compliance

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access and Control
You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Objection**: Object to certain processing of your data

### 5.2 Revocation of Access
You can revoke the Service's access to your YouTube account at any time:
1. Visit your [Google Account permissions page](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)
2. Find the YouTube Auto-Uploader application
3. Click "Remove Access"

Upon revocation:
- The Service will no longer be able to upload videos to your channel
- Your OAuth tokens will be deleted
- Previously uploaded content remains on YouTube

### 5.3 Data Export
You can request an export of your data by:
- Contacting us through the provided channels
- Using any self-service export functionality (if available)
- Accessing your data through YouTube's [Google Takeout](https://takeout.google.com/)
