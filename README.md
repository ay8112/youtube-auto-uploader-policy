# YouTube Auto Uploader Policy

## Overview

This repository contains a comprehensive policy document for organizations and content creators who use automated systems to upload videos to YouTube. The policy ensures compliance with YouTube's Terms of Service, Community Guidelines, and applicable laws while maintaining content quality and security.

## Purpose

The YouTube Auto Uploader Policy provides:
- **Compliance Guidelines**: Ensures all automated uploads adhere to YouTube's policies
- **Technical Standards**: Defines best practices for API usage and system configuration
- **Security Requirements**: Protects credentials and sensitive data
- **Quality Assurance**: Maintains high standards for automated content uploads
- **Incident Response**: Procedures for handling violations and technical issues

## Quick Start

1. Read the [complete policy document](POLICY.md)
2. Review the [Quick Reference Checklist](POLICY.md#appendix-a-quick-reference-checklist) before each upload session
3. Ensure all team members complete required training
4. Implement the technical requirements outlined in the policy
5. Set up monitoring and logging as specified

## Key Policy Areas

The policy covers 14 major areas:

1. **Purpose and Scope** - Policy objectives and applicability
2. **Compliance with YouTube Policies** - Community Guidelines, copyright, and monetization
3. **Technical Configuration** - API setup, authentication, and rate limiting
4. **Metadata Requirements** - Required and optional video metadata
5. **Scheduling and Frequency** - Upload timing and frequency guidelines
6. **Ownership and Rights Management** - Content ownership and licensing
7. **Policy Enforcement and Monitoring** - Auditing and violation handling
8. **Security and Privacy** - Credential management and data protection
9. **Incident Response** - Procedures for handling strikes and claims
10. **Quality Assurance** - Pre and post-upload verification
11. **Documentation and Training** - Requirements for documentation and staff training
12. **References and Resources** - Links to official YouTube documentation
13. **Policy Review and Updates** - Maintenance schedule
14. **Approval and Acknowledgment** - Sign-off requirements
